Ghar Ka Dabba.

Food is what reminds everyone of home, of their mothers and invokes nostalgia. The aromas, the taste takes everyone back to their roots. Food is the key to the heart and fuel for the brain.

We, at Ghar Ka Dabba, promise a similar experience by personalizing it for the students. The project aims to reduce food wastage by persoanlizing the approach to canteens and food delivery.
